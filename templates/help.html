How to get started expand_more

To get started with GrütteCloud, we suggest you create an account first.

You can do this by clicking rocket_launch Get Started! on the start page.

After entering some basic information, you will get an email to verify your account.

Once you have verified your account, you can start using GrütteCloud.

What is GrütteCloud expand_more

GrütteCloud is a web application that allows you to chat, share files, explore routes, track budgets, and much more.

It is designed to be a one-stop solution for all your needs.

GrütteCloud is built with privacy in mind, so you can be sure that your data is safe.

GrütteCloud is also open-source, so you can review the code yourself.

The following core products are available in GrütteCloud:

  • GrütteChat
  • GrütteDrive
  • GrütteMaps
  • GrütteFinance
How to use GrütteChat expand_more

GrütteChat is a chat application that allows you to communicate with other users.

To start a new chat, go to the chat page and click on the search icon.

Enter the name of the user you want to chat with and click on the user's name to start a chat.

You can also search by email address, phone number, or name.

For that, you have to enter "email:", "phone:", or "name:" followed by the search term.


When you are in a chat, you can send text messages and images.

Click on the attach_file icon or use keyboard_command_key + V to add an image to your message.

Press arrow_upward_alt or ENTER to send the message.

The freshly sent message will appear at the bottom of the chat with a schedule icon. This indicates that the message is being sent.

Once the message has been received by the server, the done icon will appear.

When the message has been read by the recipient, the done_all icon will appear.

To report the chat in case of abuse, click on the flag icon.

To delete a message you have sent, click on the respective message and then on delete Delete Message.

To block a user, go to the chat with the user and click on their name at the top. Once you're on their profile, click on the "Block User" button.


To delete a chat, go to the chat page, click on the delete icon next to the chat you want to delete.

How to use MyAI expand_more

MyAI is a chatbot that can help you with various tasks.

To start a chat with MyAI, go to the chat page and select the first chat.

You cannot delete the chat with MyAI, it will always be the first chat.

When opening the chat with MyAI, you will see a welcome message.

Tap the temp_preferences_custom icon in the top right corner to access the AI preferences menu.

Here you can change the personality of MyAI, select the AI Model or restart the chat.

MyAI currently offers the following personalities:

  • Uwu Gamer Girl (NSFW)

You can choose between the following AI models:

Please note:
The GPT-4o model is more powerful and can generate more complex responses. Additionally, it is capable of processing images you send to it (See How to use GrütteChat for more information). However GPT-4o is only available for GrütteCloud PLUS users.

How to use GrütteDrive expand_more

GrütteDrive is a file storage application that allows you to store and share files.

To upload a file, go to the drive page and click on upload area. You can also drag and drop files into the upload area.

Once the file has been uploaded, you can share it with other users by clicking on the file name and then on the ios_share icon next to the file.

Free users can upload up to 3 files.

A link will be generated that you can share with other users.

To delete a file, click on the file name and then on the delete icon.

To create a new folder, click on the create_new_folder icon.

I forgot my password expand_more

If you have forgotten your password, you can reset it on our password recovery page.

You just need your username and the email address you used to sign up.

After that you will receive an email with a link to reset your password.

But be careful, the link is only valid for 15 minutes.


If you have any problems, please contact us here.

How do I change my password expand_more

To change your password, go to the settings page and click on the key icon.

Enter your current password and then your new password.

Click on Change Password to save your new password.

How do I change my email expand_more

To change your email, go to the settings page and click on the email icon.

Enter your new email and your password.

Click on Change Email to save your new email.

How do I delete my account expand_more

If you want to delete your account, go to the settings page and click on the delete icon.

Enter your username, password, email and the reason for deletion and click on Delete account forever to delete your account.

Please note that this action is irreversible and all your data will be lost.

How do I delete my data expand_more

If you want to delete your data, the best way is to delete your account

For that, go to the settings page and click on the delete icon.

Enter your username, password, email and the reason for deletion and click on Delete account forever to delete your account.

However we may still store some data for legal reasons.

If you want to delete your data without deleting your account, please contact us here.

How is my data stored expand_more

Your data is stored on servers in Germany.

We take privacy very seriously and make sure that your data is safe.

Chat text messages are stored encrypted.

Chat images are also stored in a way that only authorized people can see them.

MyAI conversations are stored on your device and we do not have access to them.

However MyAI image files are stored on our servers for up to 14 days before being deleted.

If you have any questions about data storage, please contact us here.
