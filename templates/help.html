Help Center

How to get started

Firstly, you need to create your GrütteID.

You can do this by clicking on the "Sign Up" button on the landing page or simply click here.

You'll then be asked to enter a username, your email address, and a password.

After reviewing the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy, you can click on the "Sign Up" button.

You are then going to receive a six digit verification code via email. You'll need to enter this code on the next page.

Once you've done that, you'll be able to log in and use GrütteCloud services.

What is GrütteChat

GrütteChat is a mostly free, open-source, chat application. It is built with Python and Flask, AJAX and SQL.

Chat messages as well as user information is stored in a MySQL database.

The message content and the user passwords are encrypted before being stored in the database.

GrütteChat also has a state of the art chat-bot called MyAI built in.

You can check out the source code on GitHub.

You may also take a look at some GrütteChat promo material.

What is GrütteStorage

GrütteStorage is a cloud storage application.

Files are stored securely on our servers.

You can choose to share files directly with others via a link. You do not need to have GrütteID to view shared files

To use GrütteStorage you need to purchase GrütteCloud PLUS.

What is MyAI

MyAI is a chat-bot that is built into GrütteChat.

The bot is built using the openai API.

MyAI is equipped with conversational awareness, meaning previous messages are taken into account when generating a reply.

As a GrütteCloud PLUS subscriber, you get access to all MyAI personalities as well as longer conversations and replies.

What is GrütteCloud PLUS

GrütteCloud PLUS gives you access to the following:

• Unlock all MyAI personalities

• 4x longer MyAI conversations

• Up to 10x longer MyAI replies

• Access to GrütteStorage

• Access to all upcoming features

For free you get lifetime* access to GrütteCloud.

*Lifetime means as long as the service is available. Please refer to the Terms of Service for more information.

How can I delete my GrütteID

To delete your GrütteID and all data associated with it, please head over to your GrütteID settings or click here.

You will be asked to enter your username, password and email to confirm the deletion.

How can I change my GrütteID email

To change your GrütteID email, please head over to your GrütteID settings or click here.

You will be asked to enter your new email and your password to confirm the action.

How can I change my GrütteID password

To change your GrütteID password, please head over to your GrütteID settings or click here.

You will be asked to enter your old password and the new password to confirm the action.
