What Is GrütteChat?

GrütteChat is a new way to keep in touch with friends, family and much more...
It's free, open-source and built with Python and Flask, AJAX and SQL.

MyAI

MyAI is a state of the art AI-powered chatbot that is built into GrütteChat.
You can access MyAI right from the home page.

Real-Time Messaging

Text the ones important to you in real-time
while your messages are safe and securely encrypted.

Looks Great On Any Screen

GrütteChat is built with responsive design in mind.
Experience it on your phone, tablet, laptop or desktop computer.

GrütteStorage

A new way to store your files.
It's a fast and secure cloud storage service that is built right into GrütteCloud.
So now you can finally access files from anywhere.

Share Files

Share your files with anyone - even the once without a GrütteID.
Simply press "share" and copy the link - It's that easy!

GrütteID - One For Everything

An account for all GrütteCloud services.
Your data is safe and secure - Now with Two-Factor Authentication.
