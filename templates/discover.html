Send messages and even photos to anyone on GrütteChat. No need to worry about privacy. All messages are securely encrypted.

Your Files.
Anywhere. Anytime.

Introducing our cloud storage service, GrütteDrive.

Access and securely store Your files from anywhere in the world. Store up to 5 GB of files for free.

Share with the World.

Share Your files with anyone. No account required. Just send the link. Don't wanna share the file anymore? Just make it private with a click of a button.

Find Routes.
Blazingly Fast.

GrütteMaps Maps calculates Your route to the university within milliseconds. In fact, it's so speedy, You'll probably reach Your destination before the S-Bahn Stuttgart can even leave the station.

Click the map to set Your directions_car start and location_on destination. Or search for any place in Germany using the search bar. As a bonus, You can also choose Your current location as a starting point.
